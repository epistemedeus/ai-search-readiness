Methodology

Each company's homepage was fetched once (June 23, 2026) and scored 0-100 on six signals: AI-crawler access (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended), JSON-LD structured data, title/meta, Open Graph tags, an XML sitemap, and llms.txt. It's a point-in-time, homepage-level snapshot, not a full-site audit. Scores change as sites update.
